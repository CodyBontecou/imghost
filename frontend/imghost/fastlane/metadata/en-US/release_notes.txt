• New "Markdown (Obsidian)" link format — copy a video and paste an Obsidian-compatible video embed that plays on Mac and iOS. Images keep the familiar Markdown image-link style.
• Default media width — set a width once in Settings and it's automatically applied to image and video links across all formats.
