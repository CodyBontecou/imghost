• New "Markdown (Obsidian)" link format — copy a video and paste a working <video> tag that plays in Obsidian on Mac and iOS. Images keep the familiar ![alt](url) syntax.
• Default media width — set a width once in Settings and it's automatically applied to image and video links across all formats.
• macOS share extension now accepts a wider range of files, including image and movie URLs from Finder and other apps.
